Cricket

is a bat-and-ball game played between two teams of eleven players on a field at the centre of which is a 
22-yard pitch
with a wicket at each end, each comprising two bails balanced on three stumps. The batting side scores runs by striking the ball bowled at the wicket with the bat, while the bowling and fielding side tries to prevent this and
dismiss
each batter.
